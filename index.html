Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

Ready to take the next step toward your future career? The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub is your one-stop shop for trusted guidance, practical tools, and the latest information to help you thrive as a student and job-seeker. Whether you are just starting your exploration or getting ready to launch your career, you will find everything you need, organized for you.


Why Use This Resource?

  • Everything in One Place:
  • Stop searching endlessly, and quickly access the resources you need for resumes, interviews, networking, mentorship, and more.
  • Student-Focused Navigation:
  • Browse by topic or question. Key areas are highlighted so you can get moving right away.
  • Guidance Backed by Experts:
  • All resources are recommended and reviewed by the UMSI Career Development Office, so you can trust the advice and materials you find here.
  • Always Accessible:
  • Visit the site anytime, from any device. We’re committed to meeting the accessibility needs of all students.
  • Modern Support, UMSI Excellence:
  • We benchmark against top peer institutions and integrate UMSI’s unique support, showcasing our commitment to your success.

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized support through appointments, events, and resource guides. Visit the CDO website or find us on campus to learn more, schedule a coaching session, or just say hello. More details about the UMSI Career Development Office can also be found on the CDO's About Us page.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Who can use the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub?
  2. All UMSI students can use this hub. It is designed to support you at any stage—whether you are exploring careers, applying for internships, or preparing for full-time jobs.
  3. How is this site different from other career resources?
  4. This hub organizes trusted, UMSI-specific resources in one place. It saves you time searching so you can focus on learning and applying career skills.
  5. Is the site accessible to all students?
  6. Yes. The site follows accessibility best practices, including skip links, structured navigation, and tested screen reader compatibility. If you encounter an issue, you can report it to the Career Development Office.
  7. How do I connect with a career coach?
  8. You can schedule an appointment through the Talk to a Career Coach page. Coaching is available for resumes, networking, interviews, and more.
